He was the co-founder, chairman, and CEO of Apple;the chairman and majority shareholder of Pixar;a member of The Walt Disney Company's board of directors following its acquisition of Pixar; and the founder, chairman,and CEO of NeXT.He is widely recognized as a pioneer of the personal computer revolution of the 1970s and 1980s, along with his early business partner and fellow Apple co-founder Steve Wozniak. Steve Jobs was a visionary entrepreneur, inventor, and designer who co-founded Apple Inc., and played a major role in transforming the technology industry. He passed away on October 5, 2011, but his legacy lives on in the countless innovations he brought to the world. Born on February 24, 1955, in San Francisco, California, Steve Jobs had an early interest in electronics and entrepreneurship. In 1976, he co-founded Apple Computer, Inc. with his friend Steve Wozniak in his parents' garage. Together, they created the Apple I computer, which was the first computer with a single circuit board. Jobs' vision for a personal computer that was both user-friendly and beautifully designed led to the creation of the Macintosh computer in 1984. The Macintosh introduced the world to a graphical user interface and a mouse, which revolutionized the way people interacted with computers.
